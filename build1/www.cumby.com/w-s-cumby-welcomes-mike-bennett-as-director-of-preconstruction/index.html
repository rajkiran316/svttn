    W.S. Cumby Welcomes Mike Bennett as Director of Preconstruction

    02.14.2018

    W.S. Cumby Builders & Construction Managers is proud to announce the addition of Mike Bennett to the Estimating Department and Management Team. Mike brings over 30 years of estimating expertise with him to his new role as Director of Preconstruction at W.S. Cumby.

    “It became clear from our first meeting that Mike had a lot of complementary skills to our current team and adding him to the staff would bring us a huge amount of talent and tremendous opportunities,” said Bill Cumby, Jr., President of W.S. Cumby. “We couldn’t be more enthused about the role Mike can play in helping us grow to even greater heights.”

    Mr. Bennett is joining Chief Estimator Mike Boess’ estimating team as Director of Preconstruction, where he will lead the preconstruction process as well as business development ventures. “I have worked with Mike Bennett for years at the Associated Builders and Contractors Eastern PA Chapter and am very excited to have someone with his skills and experience join our team,” said Mike Boess.

    Prior to joining W.S. Cumby, Mike Bennett worked for twenty years as Vice President of Estimating for E. Allen Reeves, where he was responsible for supervising the Estimating Department and leading business development efforts. Mike is closely involved as a leader of Associated Builders and Contractors, Inc., where he has served as Chairman of the Eastern Pennsylvania chapter.
