    As construction manager, we apply our expertise in the areas of budget, schedule, and market trends to help ensure that your project is executed within your budget guidelines. We will work closely with you to value engineer projects and find alternative construction methods and materials that will result in quality enhancements, serviceability, and life cycle improvements. Our operations staff is well-trained and experienced in the delivery of complex, fast-paced projects while working with owners and design teams in the same partnership approach that defines us as a company.

    Case Study

    Swarthmore College Dana-Hallowell Dormitories

    W.S. Cumby was tasked with protecting the safety and quality of life of the students residing in the active Dana and Hallowell dormitories while an addition was being constructed between them. This needed to be accomplished while minimizing site disturbance and also protecting the surrounding sensitive ecological areas. A logistics plan was implemented which included installing a temporary road to divert campus traffic and isolate the construction zone from campus.

    We employ project management systems that are tailored to fit the specific needs of each project and efficiently track and control costs, material fabrication and delivery, schedules, quality, systems commissioning, and close-out.  Our field personnel leverages their experience and industry-best practices to safely deliver the highest quality, healthiest, and most efficient buildings possible.

    Case Study

    Merion Cricket Club

    In order to maintain the historic appearance of the club and not disturb its members, W.S. Cumby worked with the club to develop creative solutions for the tight site that addressed the need for contractor parking, construction deliveries, and material layout without compromising the club’s appearance.
